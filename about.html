Who We Are

Founded in 2020 by a small team of fabric nerds and city commuters, The Clothes Hangar set out to solve a simple problem: layers that look runway-ready but feel like your favorite blanket. We work with responsible mills, obsess over fit across sizes, and field-test every piece in real winter—from early morning dog walks to late-night subway rides.

Customer Experience

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the things we’re asked most.

What are your store hours?
Our studio is open Mon–Sat: 10am–6pm. We’re closed on Sundays and major holidays.
What’s your return policy?
Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery for unworn items with original tags and packaging. Final-sale items aren’t eligible. Refunds go to the original payment method.
Do you offer exchanges?
Yes—your first exchange is free for domestic orders. Start an exchange from your order confirmation email.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping arrives in 3–5 business days; expedited arrives in 1–2 business days. Processing typically takes 1 business day.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, to 40+ countries. Duties and taxes are shown at checkout where available, so there are no surprises on delivery.
Do you sell gift cards?
Absolutely—digital gift cards (delivered instantly) and physical gift cards in-store. Gift cards are final sale and never expire.
What sizes do you carry and how do they fit?
Most styles run XXS–3X with unisex fits. If you’re between sizes, we usually recommend sizing up for outerwear. A detailed size guide is coming soon.
How should I care for my garments?
Follow the care label. As a guide: knits—machine wash cold, lay flat to dry; wool coats—dry clean; puffers—machine wash gentle, tumble low with dryer balls to refluff.
How do I track my order?
You’ll get a tracking link by email once your order ships. Didn’t see it? Check spam or contact hello@clotheshangar.com.
What materials do you use?
We prioritize RWS-certified wool, recycled fills, and OEKO-TEX® dyes. Packaging is curbside-recyclable, and a take-back program is in pilot.
